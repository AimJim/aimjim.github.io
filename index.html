Game-jams

Perfect Production

Perfect Production is a game developed for the Brackeys 2025.1 Game Jam, under the theme Nothing can go wrong...
In this game, the player must check de quality of various products, allowing only the ones that meet the standard. But if 3 mistakes are made, the factory will end the player. Throughout the game, the player will be able to help other workers to start a revolution against the factory.

In this project, I was in charge of the programming the conveyor belt logic and the movement of the in-game tools.

Shut Up!

Shut Up! is a videogame developed for the Global Game Jam, under the theme Bubbles and winner of the "Most fun game" by the Francisco de VItoria University, which hosted the jam.
This games shows a roadtrip where the co-pilot is constantly speaking and getting angrier. His speech bubbles will block the vision, so the player must interact with them in order to keep driving.

In this project, I was in charge of the programming of the car and the traffic, and bugfixing.

The Scale of Life

The Scale Of Life is a videogame developed for the GMTK2024 Game Jam, under the theme "Built to Scale".
This game presents different minigames, representing from the tiniest acts of everyday life, such as having breakfast, to the biggest acts, like dreaming or observing the sky.

In this project, I programmed various minigames, and took part in testing and bugfixing.

CARnivore

CARnivore is a videogame developed for the ScreamJam 2024 Game Jam.
This game presents a road trip where you must discover what happened to the players parents. On this long journey, the car will start to break down and to behave weirdly.

I took part in this project programming the interaction and behaviour of the car, and contributing to the art by texturing and modelling.

Personal projects

Onion Ring

Onion Ring is a VR (Virtual Reality) game, started as a class project, currently in develompent.
In this game, the player must take charge of a restaurant by cooking the dishes and ensuring stocks. The town where this game takes place, does not have enough meat to deliver, so the player must use some of the customers as product, taking care of to not anger the town.

I took part in this project programming the customer behaviour and the interactions with the environment, and testing the game.

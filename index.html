Game-jams

The Scale of Life

The Scale Of Life is a videogame developed for the GMTK2024 Game Jam, under the theme "Built to Scale. This game presents different minigames, representing from the tiniest acts of everyday life, such as having breakfast, to the biggest acts, like dreaming or observing the sky.

In this project, I programmed various minigames, and took part in testing and bugfixing.

CARnivore

CARnivore is a videogame developed for the ScreamJam 2024 Game Jam. This game presents a road trip where you must discover what happened to the players parents. On this long journey, the car will start to break down and to behave weirdly.

I took part in this project programming the interaction and behaviour of the car, and contributing to the art by texturing and modelling.

Personal projects

Onion Ring

Onion Ring is a VR (Virtual Reality) game, started as a class project, actually in develompent. In this game, the player must take charge of a restaurant by cooking the dishes and ensuring stocks. The town where this game takes place, does not have enough meat to deliver, so the player must use some of the customers as product, taking care of to not anger the town.

I took part in this project programming the customer behaviour and the interactions with the environment, and testing the game.
